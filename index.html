Our Story

Lifelong Friends.
Lifelong Business.

Matt and Jeff have been friends since they were kids — literally neighbors who shared a fence growing up in Marin. When you count the lemonade stands and dog walking jobs they ran together starting at age 7, they've been in business together for over 50 years.

"In 1996 along came the opportunity to make a name for ourselves. From the first day, we were determined to make it a success."

For 29 years, it has been a rare day that one of them hasn't been on the premises making sure every customer and every car gets the care it deserves. That's the Matt & Jeff's difference — ownership that shows up.
